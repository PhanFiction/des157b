Project Overview

This AI art project explores the future of artistic creation with artificial intelligence. It aims to investigate the impact of AI on traditional artistic processes and how it reshapes the relationship between artists and their craft.

This project is based off of a story board view where we follow a student in school who uses AI generated art for his project.

Project Research

Conducted research to understand the evolving landscape of AI art and its implications for artists. Looking at interviews, surveys, and analysis of AI-generated artworks, I discovered about the challenges and opportunities faced by artists in adopting AI technologies.

Visual Design

The visual design section is aimed to reflect the fusion of art and technology inherent in AI art. I chose a modern aesthetic that harmonizes with AI-generated artworks, showing elements and futuristic motives to show innovation. The fonts I chose to use were exos, truculenta, and tilt font. These fonts made the text stick out and also fit into the theme.

User Interface

The user interface is created with animations that moves to the location of the screen as the user scrolls down. The color consist of neon theme and AI generated art such as the character and background.

There are text that would appear onto the screen. These text are a story that allows the reader to know more about an artist using AI art.

Revisions

Revision included adding animation where it would approach the screen from left side and right side as the user scrolls down. There were revisions towards contents like dialogue box where it would show up when the user scrolls down instead of it sitting down there.
